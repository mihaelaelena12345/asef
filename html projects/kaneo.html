Saint John the Theologian-Kaneo

Saint John the Theologian, Kaneo or simply Saint John at Kaneo is a Macedonian Orthodox church situated on the cliff over Kaneo Beach overlooking Lake Ohrid in the city of Ohrid, North Macedonia. The paths further on lead to the lake shores and the fisher- men settlement of Kaneo. High above the lake is erected one of the most famous churches by its location and looks, dedicat- ed to St. John. The church was built in the 13 century.
